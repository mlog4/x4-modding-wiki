## Our v2.0.0 mod stack (July 2026)

All four mods work together to give X4 9.0 players a rich dynamic-universe experience with automatic modded-faction support.

### Core mods

- **[DeadAir Scripts (mlog4 fork)](/x4-modding-wiki/mods/deadair-scripts-fork/)** — Dynamic War, Xenon Evolution, Jobs Expeditions, Station Fill, and more. The standard variant with DA custom wares.
- **[DeadAir Scripts (No DA Wares fork)](/x4-modding-wiki/mods/deadair-scripts-no-wares-fork/)** — Same feature set, stripped of DA custom wares. For VRO / X4-Reemergence / vanilla-economy players.

### Modded-faction compatibility patches

- **[Apus Compat Patch](/x4-modding-wiki/mods/apus-compat/)** — Full DA integration for Apus Stellar Treaty faction.
- **[ETW Compat Patch](/x4-modding-wiki/mods/etw-compat/)** — Full DA integration for all 3 Eternal Twilight Expansion factions.

## Quick pick guide

**Playing vanilla-ish X4 9.0 with the DA economy layer?**
→ [DeadAir Scripts (standard)](/x4-modding-wiki/mods/deadair-scripts-fork/) + DA Eco (standard)

**Playing with VRO 5.01 or X4-Reemergence?**
→ [DeadAir Scripts (No DA Wares)](/x4-modding-wiki/mods/deadair-scripts-no-wares-fork/) + optionally DA Eco (No DA Wares)

**Also playing with Apus Stellar Treaty?**
→ Add [Apus Compat Patch](/x4-modding-wiki/mods/apus-compat/)

**Also playing with Eternal Twilight Expansion?**
→ Add [ETW Compat Patch](/x4-modding-wiki/mods/etw-compat/)

All combinations tested via 5.5-hour multi-mod soaks on X4 9.0. Zero errors in the mod namespace.
