The same menu as [DA Fill → Fill Statistics Menu](../../configuration/fill/statistics/) — but reachable via **DA Information Menus** as well as via DA Fill's sub-menu.

## Why two entry points?

DA menu design consolidates diagnostic reports in `DA Information Menus` while also keeping feature-specific submenus accessible from each feature's configuration page. Fill Statistics is one of two reports that appears in both places (the other is [Progress Menus](./progress-menus/) for [Blueprint Analysis](../../configuration/blueprint-analysis/progress-menus/)).

Full documentation lives on the primary page: **[DA Fill → Fill Statistics Menu](../../configuration/fill/statistics/)** — go there for the 3 sections (Faction Account balances, Wares Added/Removed, Ships Modified) and the observed sample-save data.
