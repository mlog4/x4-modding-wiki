Parent submenu grouping the four job-related subsystems that DA adds on top of vanilla faction jobs.

> ⚠ Menu tree note: as of writing, ST and PST are documented as children of DA Jobs. If they turn out to be separate top-level items in the actual UI, they will be promoted accordingly.

## Sub-pages

- **[Jobs Expeditions](./jobs-expeditions/)** — long-range XL flagship + escort attack fleets, one per faction.
- **[Jobs SST (Situational Sector Threat)](./jobs-sst/)** — per-faction, per-sector-role patrol quotas.
- **[Station Traders (ST)](./jobs-station-traders/)** — subordinate M-class traders for player-friendly trade stations.
- **[Prod Station Traders (PST)](./jobs-prod-station-traders/)** — subordinate traders for production factories.
