**Scavenger** (`faction.scavenger`) is the **scavenger pirate faction** introduced by the Pirate DLC. They operate scrapyards, salvage operations, and a parallel economy based on scavenged materials.

## Faction profile

- **DLC**: Pirate (Tides of Avarice — `ego_dlc_pirate`)
- **Race**: Argon (primaryrace=argon)
- **Theme**: Smuggling, scavenging, scrap economy
